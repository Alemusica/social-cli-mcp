## Hashtag Strategy

### The Optimal Mix

Use 20-25 hashtags per post with this distribution:

| Size | Posts | Count | Purpose |
|------|-------|-------|---------|
| Large | >1M | 3 | Broad reach, high competition |
| Medium | 100K-1M | 7 | Good reach, moderate competition |
| Small | 10K-100K | 10 | Niche audience, lower competition |
| Micro | <10K | 5 | Very niche, can dominate |

### Rules

1. **Put hashtags in first comment** (not caption) - cleaner look
2. **Rotate hashtags** - don't use same set every post
3. **Include 2-3 brand tags** consistently: `#flutur #electronicmusic #producer`
4. **Use location tags** for local reach
5. **Never use banned/spammy hashtags** - check Instagram's guidelines

### Example Sets

**Landscape/Travel:**
```
#landscapephotography #naturephotography #earthpix #landscape_captures
#visualsofearth #discoverearth #travelphotography #wanderlust
#[location] #[country]
```

**Music Production:**
```
#musicproducer #beatmaker #producerlife #studiolife #musicproduction
#electronicmusic #producer #[daw] #[genre]
```

## Field Recording Niche

> High respect, tech-focused community. Quality over quantity.

### Primary Tags
```
#fieldrecording #sounddesign #binaural #ambisonic #soundscape
#audioengineering #locationrecording #soundart #ambientrecording
```

### Tech/Gear Tags (High Engagement)
```
#ku100 #neumann #dpa #sounddevices #zoomrecorders
#immersiveaudio #spatialAudio #3daudio #dolbyatmos
```

### Best Practices

1. **Tag gear manufacturers** - potential reposts from official accounts
2. **Use #sounddesign** - reaches film/game audio community (huge!)
3. **Include recording location** - adds context and story
4. **Show the setup** - tech community loves BTS content
5. **Cross-post to audio communities** - Reddit r/fieldrecording, Gearslutz

### Content Ideas

- Setup shots with gear visible
- Before/after: location vs. final sound design
- "What does [location] sound like?"
- Gear reviews and comparisons
- Recording techniques tutorials

## Posting Schedule

### Optimal Times (CET)

| Day | Time | Content Type |
|-----|------|--------------|
| Monday | 18:00 | Landscape/Motivational |
| Wednesday | 12:00 | Studio/Music |
| Friday | 19:00 | Sunset/Weekend vibe |
| Saturday | 11:00 | Field Recording/Technical |
| Sunday | 17:00 | Adventure/Personal |

### Frequency

- **Minimum**: 3 posts/week
- **Optimal**: 4-5 posts/week
- **Stories**: Daily when active
- **Reels**: 2-3 per week for growth

## Engagement Best Practices

### The 30-Minute Rule

Spend 30 minutes around posting time:
- 15 min before: engage with similar accounts
- 15 min after: respond to every comment

### Engagement Strategy

1. **Reply to ALL comments** within first hour
2. **Like comments** on similar accounts
3. **Leave meaningful comments** (not just emojis)
4. **Use Stories polls/questions** for engagement
5. **Share others' content** in Stories (they'll share back)

### Community Building

- Follow accounts in your niche
- Engage genuinely, not transactionally
- Collaborate with similar-sized accounts
- Join engagement groups (carefully)
