## BOOKING STRATEGIES OBSERVED

### 1. MALTE MARTEN Model (RAV Vast Focus)
- Build YouTube following with meditation content
- Partner with RAV Vast brand
- Focus on handpan festival circuit first
- Expand to wellness/retreat sector
- Self-book with occasional agency help

### 2. HANG MASSIVE Model (Mainstream Crossover)
- Viral video creates demand (pull strategy)
- Professional management handles incoming
- Concert hall circuit (churches, theaters)
- World music festival appearances
- Merchandise and album sales

### 3. KABECAO Model (Ecstatic Dance)
- Become known in ecstatic dance community
- Tour the global ED network (Bali, Portugal, Costa Rica, Amsterdam)
- Festival circuit for conscious/transformational events
- Retreat center partnerships
- Self-booking or light management

### 4. MANU DELAGO Model (Classical/Jazz)
- Collaborate with established artists (Bjork)
- Label releases (credibility)
- Concert hall bookings via agent
- Festival appearances at prestigious events
- Teaching/workshop income supplementary

### 5. SAM MAHER Model (Street to Stage)
- Build viral video presence from street
- Use social proof to open venue doors
- NYC venue circuit first (local)
- Festival applications based on virality
- Management once traction established
